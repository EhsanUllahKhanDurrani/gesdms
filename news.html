Annual Sports Day 2025

Our much‑anticipated annual sports day was held in March 2025. Students showcased their athletic talents in cricket, football, races and gymnastics. Parents and community members cheered enthusiastically as houses competed for the coveted trophy.

The day concluded with a colourful award ceremony and motivational speeches celebrating sportsmanship and teamwork. It was a memorable event that reinforced the importance of physical activity alongside academics.

State‑of‑the‑Art Computer Lab Launched

In January 2025 we proudly unveiled our newly upgraded computer lab. Equipped with high‑performance computers, interactive displays and high‑speed internet, the lab provides students with hands‑on experience in coding, graphic design and research.

The inauguration ceremony included live demonstrations of robotics projects and multimedia presentations created by our students. This facility marks a significant step forward in our commitment to integrating technology into education.

AI‑Enabled Library Inaugurated

April 2025 saw the grand opening of our AI‑enabled library. The modern space features an intuitive catalogue that suggests books based on students’ reading histories, comfortable seating and quiet study areas. Students can browse digital and physical collections seamlessly.

During the launch, the headmaster emphasised the role of libraries in cultivating curiosity and lifelong learning. Parents, teachers and students toured the facility, excited to explore a world of knowledge at their fingertips.

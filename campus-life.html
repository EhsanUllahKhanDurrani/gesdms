Campus Life

A vibrant community where learning, creativity and play come together.

Modern Computer Lab

Our fully equipped computer lab is the heart of technological learning at GES DMS. With up‑to‑date hardware and high‑speed internet, students develop digital literacy skills from an early age. Coding, robotics and multimedia projects ignite curiosity and prepare our learners for the digital future.

AI‑Powered Library

Our beautiful library blends the charm of traditional books with cutting‑edge technology. Children can explore stories in cosy reading nooks while our AI‑powered catalogue recommends new titles based on their interests. Digital resources and research tools broaden horizons and encourage independent learning.

Sports & Playgrounds

Physical well‑being is as important as academics. Our expansive playgrounds host cricket, football and hockey matches throughout the year. Qualified coaches guide students in teamwork, fair play and perseverance. Annual sports days and inter‑house competitions foster healthy competition and school spirit.

Events & Daily Routine

At GES DMS, every day is filled with engaging activities. Morning assemblies set a positive tone with Qur'an recitation, national songs and thought‑provoking messages. Throughout the year, we organise science fairs, debate competitions, cultural days and field trips. Our robotics and science clubs give students the chance to explore their passions beyond the classroom.

About GES DMS

Founded in 1939, GES Dera Malla Sing has evolved into a modern, English‑medium school offering quality education from Prep to Class VIII.

Admissions

Join our family – discover how to enrol your child at GES DMS.

Our 4‑Step Admission Process

  1. Apply: Complete the registration form and submit it to our office.
  2. Documents: Provide required documents including birth certificate, previous report card and parents’ CNIC copies.
  3. Assessment & Interview: Your child will participate in a friendly assessment and meet our headmaster.
  4. Enrollment: Upon acceptance, pay the fee and receive confirmation of your child’s place.

Required Documents

  • Completed registration form
  • Student’s birth certificate or B‑Form
  • Previous school’s report card / transfer certificate
  • Two passport‑size photographs of the student
  • Parents’/guardians’ CNIC copies

FAQs

What are the age requirements?

We accept children from the age of 4 for our Kachi (Prep) class. Placement for other grades depends on the student’s previous school record.

Do you offer scholarships or fee concessions?

A limited number of need‑based concessions are available. Please speak to our administration office during the admission process.

When does the admission cycle begin?

Admissions for the new academic year typically open in February. However, mid‑year admissions are considered based on seat availability.

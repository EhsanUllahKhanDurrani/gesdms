Academics

Excellence in education from Prep through Class VIII

Grades Offered

GES DMS offers a continuous educational journey starting from Kachi (Prep) all the way to Class VIII. Each stage builds upon the last, laying strong foundations and nurturing higher‑order thinking skills.

  • Kachi (Prep)
  • Nursery & Kindergarten
  • Primary Classes I–V
  • Middle Classes VI–VIII

Our Faculty

We are proud of our team of 18–23 dedicated teachers, each holding at least an MPhil degree in their subject area. Our faculty members are passionate educators, continually engaging in professional development to bring innovative teaching methodologies into the classroom. They mentor students with patience, respect and high expectations, ensuring that every learner reaches their full potential.

English‑Medium & Smart Learning

In 2025 we completed the transition to an English‑medium school. This shift has opened doors for our students, giving them direct access to global knowledge resources and preparing them for competitive examinations and careers. At the same time we support bilingualism by continuing to teach Urdu and Islamiat.

Our smart learning approach integrates technology into everyday instruction. Classrooms are equipped with multimedia resources and interactive content. Students regularly use the computer lab for coding, digital literacy and research assignments. An AI‑powered library catalogue helps them discover new books and develop strong information‑seeking skills.

About GES DMS

Founded in 1939, GES Dera Malla Sing has evolved into a modern, English‑medium school offering quality education from Prep to Class VIII.

About Our School

Learn about our rich history, leadership and vision for the future.

Our History

Established in 1939, Government Elementary School Dera Malla Sing has been a cornerstone of education in Sheikhupura district for over eight decades. Originally founded as an Urdu‑medium institution serving the local community, the school has grown in both size and stature while staying true to its mission of providing accessible education to every child.

Through the decades, GES DMS has weathered many challenges and embraced numerous opportunities. From modest beginnings with a single classroom, it now serves more than 700 students with fully furnished classrooms, modern facilities and a compassionate faculty.

Our dedicated team of MPhil‑qualified teachers strive to nurture curiosity, build character and inspire a lifelong love of learning. Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: To provide quality, affordable education that empowers students to become confident, compassionate and competent leaders of tomorrow.

Vision: To be a model elementary school recognised for innovative teaching, technology‑driven learning and community upliftment.

Values: We cherish integrity, respect, perseverance, creativity and collaboration. These values underpin every lesson taught and every decision made at our school.

From Urdu to English Medium

Originally an Urdu‑medium school, GES DMS has embraced the future by gradually introducing English as the primary medium of instruction. This journey began in early 2020 with intensive teacher training and curriculum development. Today all subjects from Prep to Class VIII are taught in English, giving our students the linguistic competence required to excel in higher education and global careers.

While English is our medium of instruction, we continue to celebrate and teach our mother tongue. Urdu and Islamiat remain integral parts of our curriculum, ensuring students retain a strong connection to their cultural roots.

Community Impact & Future Plans

GES DMS is more than just a school; it is a community hub. Our students participate in local service projects, cultural festivals and sports competitions that build civic responsibility. Parents and community leaders collaborate closely with school management to ensure our children receive holistic support.

Looking ahead, we plan to expand our campus facilities, introduce robotics and coding clubs, and strengthen partnerships with educational institutions worldwide. With your continued support, we will keep pushing the boundaries of what an elementary school can achieve.
